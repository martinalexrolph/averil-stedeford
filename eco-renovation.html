A pioneering eco-renoavation

Following the death of her husband Brian in 2003, Averil had an experience which shaped the rest of her life: she heard a voice, clear and authoritative, which told her to sell her house, buy a small ordinary house and make it as eco-friendly as possible.

Despite knowing very little about architecture, rennovation or construction, she embarked on a journey which culminated in her reducing the carbon emissions of an ordinary 1950s semi-detached house by an incredible 60%, a feat which won her the DIY award in the 2006 Observer Ethical Awards.
